cmake ../tensorflow_src/tensorflow/lite -DCMAKE_BUILD_TYPE=Release -DTFLITE_ENABLE_XNNPACK=ON -DCMAKE_SYSTEM_NAME=Linux -DCMAKE_SYSTEM_PROCESSOR=aarch64 -DCMAKE_C_FLAGS="-O3 -march=armv8-a -mtune=cortex-a72 -funsafe-math-optimizations" -DCMAKE_CXX_FLAGS="-O3 -march=armv8-a -mtune=cortex-a72 -funsafe-math-optimizations"

cmake --build . -j4

Notes:

Slower than FP32/FP16

YOLO models often don’t benefit from INT8 on ARM CPUs

Extra quantization/dequantization overhead

XNNPACK’s INT8 kernels aren’t always faster for this architecture
